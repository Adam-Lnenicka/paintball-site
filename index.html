Questions

Is it safe?

We strictly follow safety guidelines to ensure nobody gets hurt. Safety equipment is provided

Does it hurt?

When hit by a paintball gun, the experience can be a bit paintful. However, the safety gear ensures that no injuries occurs.

Will we recieve enough bullets?

The price of a game includes 200 bullets. However, more bullets can be purchased for additional cost.
